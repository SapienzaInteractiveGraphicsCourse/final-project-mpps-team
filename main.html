CROSSY ROAD

Welcome to Crossy Road! In this game you can choose between three levels, which correspond to three different environments each with different types of challenges. The aim is to do as many steps as possible avoiding to hit any object. You can also choose between two different times of the day in the box below.

NOTE: WE ADVISE USING CHROME OR EDGE (CHROMIUM VERSION) FOR A BETTER EXPERIENCE.
Select the hour:

Easy level - Busy town

The rabbit has to cross a noisy and busy town, populated by large buildings, trees and speeding cars. You lose whenever you hit a vehicle.

Medium level - Snow bear

The polar bear must cross a snowy landscape studded with obstacles and icy expanses. You lose whenever you hit a snowman on the snowy roads or any obstacle while the bear is sliding on ice.

Hard level - Scary Halloween

The little girl quickly slips while sitting on the green halloween slime. You are not able to stop and you lose whenever you hit any fixed or moving obstacle.

Tutorial - How to play

It is possible to go straight / turn right / turn left by simply using the up / right / left arrows on your keyboard. In the second level, if you click the up arrow while the bear is sliding on ice your speed will increase. In the third level the baby always keeps sliding and you can only use the right / left arrows to move right / left.
